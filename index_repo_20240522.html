Research

My research interest includes monitoring continuous human activities in range and time beyond micro-Doppler using distributed radar sensor networks and classification for human signature analysis applied to healthcare and security.

Distributed radar for human activity recognition (Distr-Radar)
Ronny G. Guendel, Prof. Dr. A. Yarovoy, Dr. F. Fioranelli.

The objective of this project is to investigate techniques for distributed radar and related data fusion techniques in the context of HAR (Human Activity Recognition).
